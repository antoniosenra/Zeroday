We are third-year students at the University of Chicago Law School. After taking classes related to data security, we became motivated to create a website that made it easier for people to ensure their data was safe and to get the legal recourse they're entitled to.

We are using an API that belongs to HaveIBeenPwned. Their API is what allows us to quickly retrieve information on whether a user has been compromised in a breach.
